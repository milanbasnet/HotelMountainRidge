Welcome To Our Hotel

"Hotel Mountain Ridge Bandipur" is located in Depche-dara outside the charming little town bandipur , A hill top settlement in Nepal which lately has been coming to the attention of tourism.
Bandipur is situated on an elevation of 1030 meters on a mountain saddle ,143 km to the west of kathmandu and 80 km to east of pokhara.
"Mountain Ridge Bandipur" is the very end of the small and very friendly Depche community.From your window you have got breath taing panoramic views :(The Annapurna range,A section of the himalayas).
If you are lucky during sunrise you can see the orange topped peaks.
"Mountain Ridge Bandipur" was built in the historical Newari Style -typical for this area.
Every effort has been made to ensure that it fits in with the immidate environment.
